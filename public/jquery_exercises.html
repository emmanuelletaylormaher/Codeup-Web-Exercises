Reasons Why Bigfoot is Real and Why He Should Be My Best Friend

On my 10th birthday, I wished that Bigfoot was real before blowing my candles and I firmly believe it was granted. Listed below are some compelling facts as to why we would make killer BFFS! Soon enough, you will all be agreeing with me as well.

WHY SHOULD BIGFOOT BE YOUR PAL, EMMANUELLE?

  • I've always wanted to be a cryptid
  • I am non-judgemental regarding anyone's shaving habits (or lack thereof)
  • Bigfoot is misunderstood by everyone except for me
